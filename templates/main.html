About the Website

Our log analysis tool helps you understand your system logs better. You can see patterns, find problems, and check your system's status with our simple visualization and filtering options. We offer a user-friendly interface that allows downloading simple custom analytics on the logs.

Key Features

  • Conversion of log files to structured CSV format
  • Simple filtering and sorting capabilities
  • Data visualization through plots
  • Intuitive interface for easy navigation

Getting Started

Upload your log files on the upload page of this website: localhost/upload and choose the appropriate format of your log file. Next, navigate to localhost/table to view your logs in a table format and perform simple filtering. You may generate plots from the uploaded logs by navigating to localhost/plot and choosing the type of graph to be plotted. Currently, following formats are supported:

  • Apache logs
  • Android logs
  • Syslog (Linux)

    • Note: You cannot plot a graph in localhost/plots before producing the structured CSV file from localhost/table. {% endblock content %}
